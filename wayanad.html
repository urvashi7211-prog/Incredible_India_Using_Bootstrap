Wayanad

Wayanad, located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, is a lush green paradise known for its forests, waterfalls, and wildlife. Famous for its spice plantations and tribal heritage, Wayanad offers a mix of adventure and tranquility. The Edakkal Caves with ancient carvings, Pookode Lake, and Banasura Sagar Dam are popular attractions. Trekking to Chembra Peak, exploring wildlife at Wayanad Sanctuary, and enjoying misty valleys make it a favorite destination for nature lovers. Wayanad’s culture, cuisine, and natural beauty make it one of Kerala’s most enchanting hill districts.

Wayanad Kerala ka ek enchanting hill district hai jo apne lush green tea estates, misty valleys aur dense forests ke liye mashhoor hai. Yahaan ke rolling hills aur winding trails ek natural paradise ka ehsaas dilate hain, jahan wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls aur spice plantations ek saath milkar ek mesmerizing landscape banate hain. Edakkal caves ki ancient carvings is region ke cultural depth ko dikhati hain, jabki bamboo bridges aur serene lakes isse aur bhi magical bana dete hain. Wayanad ek aisi jagah hai jo nature lovers aur adventure seekers dono ke liye ek perfect escape hai.
