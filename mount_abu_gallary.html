Mount Abu Over_all View

Mount Abu is the only hill station of Rajasthan, located in the Aravalli Range. Known for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and spiritual sites, it is a refreshing retreat from the desert heat and a popular tourist destination.

Peaceful Nature Spot (Nakki Lake)

Nakki Lake is a beautiful and peaceful spot in Mount Abu, surrounded by hills and gardens. Visitors enjoy boating, evening walks, and the serene atmosphere of this natural attraction.

Historical Place (Dilwara Temples)

The Dilwara Temples are world-famous Jain temples known for their exquisite marble carvings and architectural brilliance. Built between the 11th and 13th centuries, they are a spiritual and artistic treasure of Mount Abu.

Local Market (Mount Abu Bazaar)

Mount Abu Bazaar is a lively market offering handicrafts, Rajasthani textiles, souvenirs, and local snacks. It reflects the colorful culture of the region and is a favorite spot for tourists to shop.

Famous Festival (Summer Festival)

The Summer Festival of Mount Abu is celebrated every year in May, featuring folk music, dance, boat races, and cultural programs. It highlights the traditions and vibrancy of Rajasthan in a hill station setting.

Famous Local Food Items (Meals → Gatte ki Sabzi)

Gatte ki Sabzi is a traditional Rajasthani dish enjoyed in Mount Abu. Made with gram flour dumplings cooked in a spicy yogurt-based curry, it is a flavorful and authentic meal of the region.
