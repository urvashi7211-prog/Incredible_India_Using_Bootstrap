Thekkady

Thekkady, located in Kerala’s Idukki district, is a nature lover’s paradise famous for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Known for its lush forests, spice plantations, and serene Periyar Lake, Thekkady offers opportunities for boating, trekking, and wildlife spotting. Visitors can see elephants, tigers, and diverse bird species in their natural habitat. Spice gardens and traditional Kerala cuisine add cultural richness, while eco-tourism activities make Thekkady a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility.

Thekkady, nestled in the Idukki district of Kerala, is a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Best known for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, it offers thrilling experiences like boat rides on Periyar Lake where elephants, deer, and even tigers can be spotted along the forested banks. Thekkady’s lush spice plantations of cardamom, pepper, and cinnamon add fragrance to the air, making it a paradise for explorers. Trekking trails, bamboo rafting, and eco-tourism activities highlight its adventurous spirit, while the serene landscapes provide peace and relaxation. With its blend of wilderness, spice-scented charm, and cultural richness, Thekkady stands as one of Kerala’s most captivating destinations.
